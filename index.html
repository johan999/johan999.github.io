About

My name is Johan Eliasson. I am a student living in Norrköping and studies my fifth year in Master of Science in Media Technology and Engineering at Linköpings University, campus Norrköping.

My main interest and course focus lies within game design, artificial intelligence and information visualization.

Knowledge I gained from other course outside of my main focus contains; scientific visualization, VR technology, project manegement, agile work-methodology, UX-design, document structures, special effects and evaluation of IT-products.
